DrugRadar 1.1 — App Review demonstration videos

Recorded on an iPad Air 11" simulator (the review device class) from the actual native app. These demonstrate the native, offline functionality referenced in the App Review reply and notes.

1. Offline full-text label comparison (EU SmPC + EU-vs-US)

Select two medicines → "Compare labels" → full Summaries of Product Characteristics render side by side. One column is then switched EU→US, surfacing the US-only Boxed Warning next to the EU label. A structured transformation of official label PDFs — not a web view.

2. The same comparison working fully offline

Wi-Fi is switched off (status bar shows no connectivity); the full comparison stays rendered, and a cold relaunch still loads the entire catalogue from the on-device bundle. A web browsing experience is impossible offline.
